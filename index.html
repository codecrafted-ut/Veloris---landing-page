FAQ

Questions answered.

How does the 7-day free trial work?
Download Veloris and sign in — you get full Plus-level access for 7 days. Unlimited rules, all analytics, all habits, and 5 Negotiation AI sessions/day. No credit card required, ever.
What happens when my trial expires?
You silently drop to the free tier — no hard lock screen, no drama. You keep 2 Negotiation AI sessions/day and basic blocking. All your data — streaks, habits, sessions — stays intact. Upgrade anytime to get everything back.
Do I need a credit card to start?
No. Just download and go. You only enter billing details when you choose to upgrade.
How does app blocking work?
Veloris monitors running processes every 3 seconds and force-kills any that match your blocked list. System-level — not a browser extension. You can't bypass it by switching browsers or apps.
Does website blocking work on all browsers?
Yes. It edits the Windows hosts file — completely browser-agnostic. Chrome, Firefox, Edge, all blocked simultaneously. Requires running Veloris as Administrator to enable.
What is Negotiation AI exactly?
An AI discipline coach whose only job is keeping you working. It uses tough-love responses and knows your active rules, goals, and streak. Free users get 2 sessions/day, Plus gets 5/day, Pro gets unlimited. It will not go easy on you.
What does Parental PIN do exactly?
A 4+ digit PIN that locks all Veloris settings. No rules can be changed, no features disabled, without entering it first. Perfect for parents setting up kids' study mode — or for anyone who wants to remove their own escape hatch. Pro only.
Is there a discount for yearly billing?
Yes — paying yearly saves you significantly. Plus goes from $8/mo to $80/yr (~$6.67/mo). Pro goes from $15/mo to $150/yr (~$12.50/mo). Click the Yearly tab on the pricing section above.
Is my data private?
All focus data and analytics are stored locally on your device using SQLite. We don't sell your data. Auth is handled by Firebase. Full details in our Privacy Policy.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Veloris → Settings → Subscription → Cancel. Your plan stays active until end of the billing period. 3 clicks, no emails, no guilt-trip retention screens. Or email veloris.support@gmail.com with your Gumroad Order ID.
