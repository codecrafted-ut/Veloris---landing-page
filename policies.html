Privacy Policy

We collect the minimum data needed to make Veloris work. Your focus data stays on your device.

🔒 Short version: Your focus sessions, habits, and analytics are stored locally on your device using SQLite. We never sell your data. We don't track your productivity data on our servers.

1. What data we collect

Veloris collects two categories of data:

  • Account data (Firebase): Your email address, display name, and profile photo (if you sign in with Google). This is handled by Firebase Authentication and stored on Firebase servers with industry-standard encryption.
  • App usage data: Which features you use and when, for the purpose of improving the app. This is anonymous and not linked to your identity.

2. What data we do NOT collect

  • Your focus session history, times, or durations — stored locally only
  • Which apps or websites you blocked
  • Your habit completions or discipline scores
  • Your Negotiation AI conversations
  • Your screen content or keystrokes — ever

3. How your data is stored

All productivity data (sessions, habits, rules, analytics) is stored in a SQLite database on your local device at %AppData%\Veloris\veloris.db. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

4. Third-party services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication only. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data retention

Your local data is retained on your device until you uninstall Veloris or manually delete the database. Your Firebase account data is retained until you delete your account from Settings → Account → Delete Account.

6. Your rights

  • Access: You can view all your data at any time within the app.
  • Export: Pro users can export their data as CSV from Analytics.
  • Delete: Delete your account anytime from Settings → Account → Delete Account. This removes your Firebase auth record. Your local SQLite data can be deleted by uninstalling the app.
  • Correction: Update your display name and email in Settings → Profile.

7. Children's privacy

Veloris is not directed at children under 13. Parental PIN is a feature designed for parents to control the app on behalf of their children — the parent remains the account holder. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to this policy

We'll notify you of any significant changes via in-app notification and by updating the date at the top of this page.
